Our Story

The Golden Crust is a family run bakery that has been serving the Dundee community for over 50 years. We pride ourselves on our delicious and fresh bakery products that are made with love and care. Our bakery is open 7 days a week and we are always happy to see new and returning customers. We have a wide range of products to suit all tastes and dietary requirements. We look forward to seeing you soon!
